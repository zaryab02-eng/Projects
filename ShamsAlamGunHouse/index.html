About Our Master Gunsmith

Licensed Professional

Three Generations of Excellence

Founded by my grandfather in 1952, Heritage Gunsmithing has been serving the community with professional firearm services for over 70 years. I'm a third-generation licensed gunsmith, carrying on the family tradition of precision craftsmanship and honest service.

With decades of experience in firearm restoration, repair, and customization, I take pride in treating each weapon with the respect and attention it deserves. Whether it's a family heirloom or a modern sporting rifle, every piece that comes through our doors receives the same meticulous care.

Our Expert Services

Professional gunsmithing services with attention to detail and craftsmanship

Handgun Repair

Complete repair and maintenance services for all handgun types including revolvers and semi-automatics.

  • Trigger work and adjustment
  • Sight installation and alignment
  • Barrel replacement and threading

We service all major brands including Smith & Wesson, Colt, Glock, Sig Sauer, Ruger, and more. Our services include precision trigger work, custom sight installation, barrel replacement, complete overhauls, and performance modifications.

Starting Prices:

Basic cleaning & inspection: $35

Trigger work: $85

Complete overhaul: $150

Shotgun Services

Specialized shotgun repair, modification, and restoration services for hunting and sporting shotguns.

  • Choke tube installation
  • Barrel threading and porting
  • Stock refinishing and fitting

Professional shotgun services including choke tube installation, barrel threading, stock refinishing, action tuning, and complete restorations. We work on all gauge shotguns from antique side-by-sides to modern semi-automatics.

Starting Prices:

Choke tube installation: $45

Stock refinishing: $120

Action tuning: $95

Custom Engraving

Artistic custom engraving services to personalize your firearms with beautiful, lasting designs.

  • Hand engraving and scrollwork
  • Laser engraving services
  • Custom design consultation

Beautiful hand engraving, precision laser engraving, and custom scrollwork. Perfect for commemorative pieces, gifts, or adding personal touches to your favorite firearms. We work with traditional and modern engraving techniques.

Starting Prices:

Simple laser engraving: $25

Custom scrollwork: $150

Full hand engraving: $300

Vintage Restoration

Expert restoration of antique and vintage firearms to their original glory while maintaining authenticity.

  • Period-correct restoration
  • Metal refinishing and bluing
  • Wood restoration and repair

Authentic restoration techniques using period-correct methods, metal refinishing, wood restoration, and parts fabrication. We specialize in Civil War era through WWII firearms, maintaining historical accuracy and value.

Consultation Required

Each restoration is unique and priced individually based on condition, rarity, and required work.

Common questions about our gunsmithing services

Simple repairs and cleaning typically take 3-5 business days. More complex work like restorations or custom engraving can take 2-4 weeks depending on the scope of work. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline when we evaluate your firearm.
